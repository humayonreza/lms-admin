Educators can effectively utilize images and media in online learning materials to engage learners, enhance understanding, and create a visually appealing and inclusive learning environment. Some guidelines are as follows:
  • Use images and media that are accurate and factually correct
  • Respect copyright laws and obtain proper permissions or use royalty-free images and media. Give credit to the original source when necessary.
  • Optimize images and media for online use to minimize loading times and bandwidth usage. Compress images without compromising quality to improve website performance.
  • Be mindful of the ethical implications of using certain images or media. Respect cultural sensitivities, privacy rights, and avoid perpetuating stereotypes or biases.
